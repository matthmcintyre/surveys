SURVEY TITLE: Venous Thromboembolism Follow-Up
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: This survey is designed to find out more about your health as well as your attitudes and understanding of the causes and consequences of Venous Thromboembolism (VTE). VTE is a condition in which blood clots form in veins (deep vein thrombosis, DVT), and occasionally travel through the bloodstream to the lungs (pulmonary embolism, PE).

This survey is a follow-up to our VTE Baseline survey which you completed approximately 9 months ago in 2015 between March 19th and May 1st.
