SURVEY TITLE: Rosacea
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: Rosacea, sometimes referred to as adult acne, is a common skin condition among men and women 30 and older. By answering the questions in this survey you will help us investigate genetic variants involved in the development of rosacea. For this study, we need people with rosacea and without rosacea to complete this survey.

Estimated time to complete: 2-5 minutes

This survey was developed in collaboration with Stanford researcher, Anne Lynn S. Chang, MD, Instructor of Dermatology at Stanford University School of Medicine and Director of Dermatological Clinical Trials.
