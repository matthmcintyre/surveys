13. What is your resting heart rate?
To ensure a precise measure, please make sure you have been seated for at least five minutes before attempting to take your heart rate.
Instructions on how to take your resting heart rate:

1. Choose the hand you are most comfortable with.
2. Hold up the index (pointer) and middle finger.
3. Place the pads of those two fingers onto to the thumb-side of the wrist on the other hand, slightly below where the wrist bends towards the arm.
4. Press lightly until you feel a pulsating thump.
5. Set your stopwatch or timer to record 60 seconds.
6. Record the number of times you felt a pulsating thump within 60 seconds. (Example: 86 thumps in 60 seconds would be reported as 86)
