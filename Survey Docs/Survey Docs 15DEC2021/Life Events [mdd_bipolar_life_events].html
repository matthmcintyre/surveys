SURVEY TITLE: Life Events
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: Your answers to the following questions will help us measure the impact of potentially distressing or traumatic events you may have experienced in your lifetime.

Please note that you will be asked about your experiences with events such as disasters, accidents, assault (including sexual assault), combat zones, serious illness, and violent death. Some of these questions may be difficult to answer and may cause emotional distress. Your answers to these questions are voluntary and you can skip any question you do not wish to answer.

Estimated time to complete this survey: 3-4 minutes.
