SURVEY TITLE: Hand Arthritis
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: This survey asks about your experience with hand arthritis. This information will help us keep track of how you feel about your hand and how well you are able to perform your usual activities.

Your responses to this survey will help scientists at 23andMe better understand how genetic differences are related to the impact of hand arthritis.

Estimated time to complete: 5-7 minutes.
