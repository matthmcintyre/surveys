SURVEY TITLE: Your Birth
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: Preterm birth is a significant cause of morbidity worldwide. This survey is designed to gather information about your mother's pregnancy with you. The underlying genetics of preterm birth may be explained by the maternal genetics, fetal genetics, or a combination between the two. Your answers will help us develop a better understanding of the fetal genetics which contribute to this important condition.

This study is in collaboration with the Cincinnati Children's Hospital.

Estimated time to complete: 2-3 minutes
