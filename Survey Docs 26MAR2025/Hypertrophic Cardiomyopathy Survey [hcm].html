SURVEY TITLE: Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy Survey
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: Hypertrophic cardiomyopathy (HCM) is a rare heart condition with symptoms ranging from no symptoms, to mild, to life-threatening. Many patients may not obtain accurate diagnoses for some time, or well into adulthood. In some cases, HCM is related to rare genetic variants but not in others. 23andMe is working with MyoKardia to better understand the experiences of people living with HCM. By answering this survey, you will help us understand your experience with HCM and help drive research forward.
