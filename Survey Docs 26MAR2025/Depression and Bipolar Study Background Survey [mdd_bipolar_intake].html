SURVEY TITLE: Depression and Bipolar Study Background Survey
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: Welcome to our studies on depression and bipolar disorder. This is the first of nine study sessions that we will ask you to complete over the next nine months. In your first study session we ask that you complete three activities.

Estimated time to complete this session: 15-17 minutes

Activity 1 is a background survey where we will ask you questions about yourself, your lifestyle, and your family history. There will also be questions about traumatic childhood events. Some of these questions may be difficult to answer and may cause emotional distress. We ask these questions to capture the most complete picture of your experiences. Your answers to these questions are voluntary and you can skip any question you do not wish to answer. This first activity should take 10-12 minutes to complete.

Activity 2 is a short survey about your medications and recent mood. This survey should take three minutes to complete.

Activity 3 is a cognitive test which should take two minutes to complete. This test can only be taken on a computer (laptop or desktop) - it is not available on mobile devices. We will give you instructions and a practice session before the test. Remember there are no right or wrong answers!
