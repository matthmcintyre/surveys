SURVEY TITLE: Pyruvate Kinase Deficiency Survey
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: Pyruvate kinase (PK) deficiency is a rare genetic disorder in which red blood cells break down too quickly, leading to chronic anemia. A person must have two variants in the PKLR gene, or two copies of a variant, in order to have this condition. This survey is a collaboration with Agios Pharmaceuticals, Inc. and aims to expand our understanding of this condition. Estimated time to complete: 3-10 minutes
