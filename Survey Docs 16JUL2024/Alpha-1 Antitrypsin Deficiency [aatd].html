SURVEY TITLE: Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: Alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency (AATD) is a genetic condition that increases the risk of lung and liver disease, particularly among those who smoke or use alcohol. This condition is often difficult to diagnose due to its similarity to other lung and liver conditions. The purpose of this survey is to understand health history and lifestyle behaviors among people who may or may not have lung or liver disease, including AATD.
