SURVEY TITLE: PD Off Label Drug Use
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: This survey will help us understand the prevalence of off-label drug prescription in Parkinson's disease (PD). Off-label drug prescription includes the prescription of a drug to treat a condition different from those for which it has been approved. This is accepted practice in medicine, but there is little information on how common it is in the treatment of PD. We are collaborating with The Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson's Research and The Cure for Parkinson's Trust on this research.

Depending on your responses, you may be contacted for additional research opportunities in this area. Most people completed this survey in less than 5 minutes.
This survey is not an endorsement of off-label drug use. Off-label drug use is a common practice in medicine, but generally less is known about the safety and effectiveness of drugs in unapproved indications. 23andMe encourages individuals with Parkinson's to talk with their physicians regarding treatment decisions.
