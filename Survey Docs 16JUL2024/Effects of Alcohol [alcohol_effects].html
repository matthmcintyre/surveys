SURVEY TITLE: Effects of Alcohol
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: We are interested in learning how genes influence how people respond to alcohol. This survey asks questions about your responses at 3 different points in time. For these questions a drink is defined as 12 ounces of beer, 1.5 ounces (or a shot) of 80 proof beverages (like vodka, whisky, or gin), or 5 ounces of most wines (a 750ml bottle has about 5 standard drinks).

Estimated time to complete: 3-5 minutes.
