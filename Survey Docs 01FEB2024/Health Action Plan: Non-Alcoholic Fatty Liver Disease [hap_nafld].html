Metabolic syndrome (sometimes called insulin resistance syndrome) is a group of conditions that, when present together, can increase the risk for liver or heart disease, stroke, diabetes, and other serious health issues. It includes high blood pressure, high blood sugar, high triglycerides, high LDL "bad" cholesterol, low HDL "good" cholesterol and a large waistline associated with excess abdominal fat ("apple" body shape).
