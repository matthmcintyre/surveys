SURVEY DESCRIPTION: Post-acute COVID-19 syndrome (PACS), also called 'Long COVID' or 'Long Haulers', has emerged as a condition of concern in an estimated 10-25% of those who have experienced SARS-CoV-2 infection. While there is much to learn about this condition, including the underlying causes, extent of disability, and effective treatments, this survey was designed to explore genetic associations which may be associated with PACS. Thank you for your continued participation in this research.
