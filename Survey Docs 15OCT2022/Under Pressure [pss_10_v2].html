SURVEY TITLE: Under Pressure
SURVEY DESCRIPTION: Stress can have a significant impact on various aspects of our lives. Long-term exposure to high levels of stress hormones can have detrimental effects on our health as well. By taking this survey, you can help us better understand how stress interacts with other factors to affect the risk for a variety of diseases.

Estimated time to complete: 5 minutes
This survey, entitled the Perceived Stress Scale, was developed by Sheldon Cohen and originally published in its full 14-item form in Cohen et al. "A global measure of perceived stress" Journal of Health and Social Behavior 24:386-396 (1983). The 10-item form was published in Cohen and Williamson. "Perceived stress in a probability sample of the United States." In Spacapan and Oskamp (eds.), The social psychology of health: Claremont Symposium on applied social psychology. Newbury Park, CA: Sage. (1988).
